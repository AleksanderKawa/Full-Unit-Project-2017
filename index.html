What is a Project?

Project Management has various definitions for instance, “A project is a temporary endeavour undertaken to create a unique product, service, or result.” (PMI, PMBOK Guide), “A temporary organisation that is needed to produce a unique and predefined outcome or result at a prespecified time using predetermined resources” or “A management environment that is created for the purpose of delivering one or more business product to a specified Business Case” (Prince2).
